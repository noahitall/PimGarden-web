Privacy Policy

Introduction

At PimGarden, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is handled when you use our app.

The Short Version

PimGarden does not collect or transmit any user data whatsoever.

  • We don't collect analytics
  • We don't have servers that store your data
  • We don't have access to any information you enter in the app
  • All your data stays on your device

Information Collection and Use

PimGarden is designed to be completely offline and privacy-respecting. All information you enter in the app (contacts, interactions, photos, notes, etc.) is stored locally on your device only. This information is never transmitted off your device.

App Permissions

PimGarden may request the following permissions:

  • Contacts: To import existing contacts (optional). The app processes these locally and never uploads them.
  • Camera/Photos: To add photos to contact profiles (optional). Photos remain on your device.
  • Storage: To store app data locally on your device.

Email Collection

If you choose to subscribe to updates through our website, we collect only your email address. This is stored securely and used solely to send you occasional updates about the app. We don't share your email with anyone.

Data Security

Within the app, your data can be encrypted for additional security. This encryption happens locally on your device. The encryption keys are either generated locally or derived from your passphrase, ensuring that only you can access your data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about our Privacy Policy, contact us at: contact@pimgarden.com
