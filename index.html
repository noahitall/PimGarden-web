About PimGarden

PimGarden was created with a simple goal: to provide a powerful contact management tool that respects your privacy. In today's world where data privacy is increasingly important, PimGarden offers a refreshing alternative to cloud-based contact managers.

With PimGarden, you can organize your personal and professional relationships into people, groups, and topics. Track your interactions, set reminders, and use tags to create a sophisticated contact management system - all without your data ever leaving your device.

About the Developer

PimGarden is developed by Noah Zitsman, a privacy-focused software developer committed to creating tools that respect user data and privacy. Noah believes that powerful technology doesn't have to come at the expense of personal privacy.
